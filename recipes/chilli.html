Description

This recipe does not have many recipes so it is very economic. Even though is has a few ingredients you will be suprised by how good it is.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can xhilli beans, drained
  • 2 tablespoons chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce
  4. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper
