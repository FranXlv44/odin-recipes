Description

If I were to be asked what my favourite vegetablecomination is it would be avocado with tomato. And what makes that combination better? That would be putting it between bread with an egg to make the guacamole sandwich.

This sandwich is one of the best sandwiches I make. It is simple to make and every delicious.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 small tomato
  • 1 egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the avocado, remove the seed and cut it into cubes. Cube the tomatoes into medium size cubes
  2. Put the cubed tomato and avocado into a bowl and mix them together
  3. Add a pinch of salt and black pepper to the guacamole
  4. Fry the egg sunny side up
  5. Take 2 slices of bread and put the guacamole and the egg between them
  6. Toast the sandwich until it looks golden brown
  7. Enjoy!
